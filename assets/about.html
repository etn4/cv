About Me

My journey began back in 2014 when I first attended the University of Washington - Seattle for where I firsted garnered interest in coding. I would go on to graduate from the University of Minnesota - Twin Cities with a degree in statistics and computer science in 2018.

Fast forward 7 months later, I started my career as a ServiceNow Administrator and eventually becoming a ServiceNow consultant. With certifications such as the mainline System Administrator and Application Developer, I gained experience in various areas of the platform such as Flow Designer, App Engine Studio, UI Builder, with a solid foundation of client-side and server-side scripting, business rules, and UI Policies

However, I never gave up on wanting to get better at coding and web development in general. I took a Udemy coding bootcamp where I honed my skills at various areas of web development including, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, React, and much more - everything you see on this site is the result of my hard work and never giving up!
